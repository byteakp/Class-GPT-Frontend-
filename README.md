## 💡 What's ClassGPT?

ClassGPT is your ✨kawaii✨ AI-powered study buddy! Whether you're cramming for finals or just curious about Machine Learning, ClassGPT creates:

📓 Notes | 🖼️ Slides | ❓ MCQs — **Automatically!**
